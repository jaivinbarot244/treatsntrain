            • FAQS

            Have More Questions? Find Here

            What age range is considered "puppy" and "adult" for your training programs?

            My puppy training programs are designed for dogs under 8 months of age, while my adult dog training is tailored for dogs over 8 months.

            What training methods do you use?

            I primarily use positive reinforcement training methods, focusing on rewards, praise, and encouragement to reinforce desired behaviors. My approach is humane and effective.

            How long are the training sessions, and how frequently should they occur?

            My training sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, depending on the program and the dog’s attention span. The frequency varies but is often recommended 4-5 times per week for optimal results.

            Do you offer in-home training sessions?

            Yes, I offer in-home training sessions for the convenience of both you and your dog. If you prefer this option, please inquire about availability and pricing for in-home training sessions.

            Are your dog training programs suitable for all dog breeds?

            Yes, my dog training programs are designed to be suitable for all dog breeds.

            Can you help with specific behavioral issues in adult dogs?

            Absolutely. My adult dog training programs address a range of behavioral issues, including aggression, anxiety, and other challenges. I customize the training to suit the individual needs of each dog.

            How long does it take to see results from your training?

            Results vary based on the dog’s temperament and the training program. Typically, you’ll notice positive changes within a few weeks. Consistency is key!

            Is there a guarantee that my dog will be fully trained by the end of the program?

            While I cannot guarantee specific outcomes, my training programs are designed to equip both dogs and owners with the skills needed for success. Consistency and ongoing reinforcement at home play a crucial role in the training process.
